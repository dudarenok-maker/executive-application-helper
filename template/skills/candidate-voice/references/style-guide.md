**Language and formatting**
- Quantify wherever possible: percentages, team sizes, budgets, timeframes, delivery velocity. Numbers do the work that adjectives cannot.
- Bold key role titles, initiatives, and results — this aids skim-reading by time-poor panels.
- Use short bullet clusters for achievement lists, but frame them with a topic sentence that gives context. Never lead with bullets — always a sentence first.
